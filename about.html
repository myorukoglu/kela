About us

Delivering insights from millions of patients to every patient.

"We are challenging the status quo by encouraging faster, smarter use of data by surgeons."

Welcome from Founder & CEO, Bora Chang, MD.

We started as a group of surgeons, statisticians and engineers at Duke University who wanted to make surgery safer. A few years later, KēlaHealth provides insights on thousands of patients and has grown to become a national thought leader in data science and surgery care.

Our Leadership

Bora Chang, MD guest speaker at Precision Medicine world conference in Silicon Valley, CA in 2019.

Bora Chang, MD

CEO & Co-Founder

Bora is a physician with a passion for impacting healthcare systems through innovation and cutting-edge technologies. She enjoys bridging the gap between artificial intelligence and actionable intelligence at the bedside of surgical care. She is a healthcare policy writer, speaker, advocate and educator.

Murat Yorukoglu

CTO & Chief Compliance Officer

Murat has been building software products for 25 years and has worked at venture backed startups in Silicon Valley for nearly a decade. He's been lucky enough to support missions that are meaningful to him, like telehealth, behavioral health, food traceability, supporting cancer patients, and machine learning for surgeons.

David Burdick

Head of Engineering

Over the last 15 years, David has helped solve some of the most perplexing problems in research and healthcare delivery. David has deep expertise in workflow design, data engineering and data analytics has been a key architect of applications that deploy machine learning and AI decision support in clinical settings. His work has spanned both academia and industry in biotechnology, translational medicine and clinical delivery.
