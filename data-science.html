Our hand-picked Clinical and Data Advisory Board (CDAB) is comprised of some of the country’s top industry experts, including clinicians, researchers, and professionals within their respective surgical or data specialties.

Erich S. Huang, MD, PhD

Quality Advisor

Erich is Chief Data Officer for Quality in Duke Health and Director of Duke's Center for Health Data Science, Duke's Data Science Accelerator, Assistant Dean and first faculty recruit for the Division of Translational Bioinformatics in the Department of Biostatistics & Bioinformatics, Assistant Dean of Biomedical Informatics and Director of Product Development in the Office of Research Informatics.

George Northup

Software Advisor

George has built 4 companies with over $2 billion of cumulative revenues, while raising over $500 million of capital through IPO's or venture capital. He has worked for top VCs like Kleiner Perkins, DFJ, Foundry Group, Mobius Ventures.
